The most frequent questions answered below

  • How to wash custom clothes?

    Wash at 30-40 degrees with detergents meant for delicate clothes, at washing in the washing machine, choose a gentle (delicate) mode, turn the item inside out before washing

  • What are the care guidelines
    for a clothing with a print?

    There are several simple tips on how to keep a print fresh and bright: 1. wash on temperatures about 30-40 degrees with detergents meant for delicate clothes. 2. Turn your clothes inside out and wash it separately from other items. 3. Iron the drawing from the backside.

  • What types of fabric do you work with?

    These prints are suitable for almost all types of fabric, denim jacket, cotton t-shirt, sweatshirt, leather shoes, winter jacket – are perfect for fabric painting; when you order a painting you can specify the compatibility of paint with the type of paint you chose, specifying the type of fabric, or writing to us.

  • What determines the manufacturing time?

    Production time depends on the complexity and size of the drawing, and an idea (whether you have a sketch for a print) Standard delivery time ranges from 5-7 days, if necessary, we create sketches and help with your ideas, so that increasing the manufacturing time.

  • What kind of paint do you use?

    At our work we use only specialized acrylic paints based on water-based acrylic dispersion, which will not deteriorate and will not be erased after washing.

  • Can I have my own item painted?

    Our work implies painting on any clothing, shoes and accessories. You can order the drawing you’ve chosen and we will implement it. Whether it's sneakers or your favorite hoodie, it's very important that everything should be clean and ironed before drawing, otherwise we won't be able to get down to work
